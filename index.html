Unaddressed From Last Meeting

The main idea of last meeting was a Minecraft Hunger Games.
The idea was brought up by Spencer Dinwiddie after he saw a JeromeASF video and figured we could benefit from the same idea.
We ended off the discussion beginning to talk about the downsides of the idea, specifically that the target audience had largely transitioned over to Fortnite after Professor Powell was added to the item shop.

New Ideas Presented

Cops and Robbers

Participants would split off into two teams: one being cops, and the others being robbers. They would then engage in the role play of their designated roles with robbers trying to escape and cops trying to prevent their escape.

Lucky Block Mod Showcase

Three teams of two are given half a stack of lucky blocks from the lucky blocks mod and after breaking all of their lucky blocks, the teams will fight in the middle arena will all the items they have obtained.
